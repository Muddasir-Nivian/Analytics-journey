## Week 1 Audit - Basic Formulas
**Date:** July 2026  
**Task given to AI:**
Solve 8 business questions on a retail sales dataset using basic Excel Formulas.

**Results:**  
AI answered 7 out of 8 questions correctly.  
87.5% accuracy.

**The error - Q6 Average Unit Price:**  
Correct answer: 26.454  
AI answer: 49.05

**Error type:**
Conceptual misinterpretation. AI did not make a syntax error or reference the wrong column.  
It solved a different calculation entirely - The question was simple and clear, Find The Average of Unit price.  
Column's name was clear "Unit Price" but what I guess it just took the Unit Price as a random command rather than column refrence.  
It shows AI can have Languag, Refrence understading or Indication Difficulties to understand.

**Why this matters:**
This kind of error is the most dangerous in real business analysis.   
The formula runs without error.   
The number looks plausible.  
No warning appears.  
Only someone who understands the data and businesss question can catches it.  

**Lesson:**
AI accuracy on simple formulas is approximately 87.5%.  
As formula complaxity increases this rate will likely decrease further.   
AI outputs always require validation by someone who understans the underlying data logic.  

## Week 2 Audit - Basic Charts and storytelling  
**Date:** July 2026  

**Task given to AI:**  
Create four charts.  
1 January performance  
2 February performance  
3 Product Category performance comparison  
4 Cities performance comparison 

**Results:**  
Formula implementation and calculation is almost correct but placement and Visualization seems amateur.

**Error type:**  
Placement of charts is pretty confusing and Unsettling and AI has combined some Chart values with colour that represents something completely opposite.

**Why this matters:**  
Colour theory isn't that important in storytelling and Charts but understanding its basics are.  
Different colour influences differently in human brain, Having a different kind of value and merging it with the colour which is used for something completely different or opposite can confuse the Client or Professionals.

**Lesson:**  
AI can create charts and execute formulas and calculations behind it almost correctly but it isn't restricted or responsible for human psychology yet, AI can't execute the tasks in a way that intersects Influencing human psychology and Data yet.
